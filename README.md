# Carbon Credit Protocol (Soroban | Stellar)

## Overview

The Carbon Credit Protocol is a decentralized smart contract built on the Stellar network using Soroban. It enables the issuance, management, and transfer of carbon credits in a transparent and trustless environment.

This project aims to modernize carbon credit systems by leveraging blockchain technology to ensure verifiable ownership, prevent double counting, and improve accessibility.

---

## Problem

Traditional carbon credit systems face several limitations:

* Lack of transparency in issuance and tracking
* Centralized control over credit distribution
* Risk of double counting and fraud
* Limited participation for individuals and small organizations

---

## Solution

This protocol introduces a blockchain-based system where carbon credits are:

* Issued on-chain
* Tracked transparently
* Transferred securely between participants
* Stored in an immutable ledger

---

## Core Functionality

### Minting

Authorized users can mint new carbon credits representing verified carbon offset activities.

### Transfer

Users can transfer carbon credits directly to other accounts without relying on intermediaries.

### Balance Tracking

The contract allows querying of carbon credit balances for any address.
